People Groups

People can access relevant information as per the user group to which they belong. Each people group has specific requirements from the Government for their development. We have attempted to address this need by providing relevant and useful information. Links for specific user groups are provided at one place. The list of people groups may not be exhaustive as of now but we promise to keep increasing the list.
