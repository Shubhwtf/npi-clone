India at a Glance

    

India is one of the oldest civilizations in the world with a kaleidoscopic variety and rich cultural heritage. has achieved
all-round socio-economic progress since its Independence. As the 7th largest country in the world, India stands apart from
the rest of Asia,marked off as it is by mountains and the sea, which give the country a distinct geographical entity. Bounded by
the Great Himalayas in the north, it stretches southwards and at the Tropic of Cancer, tapers off into the Indian Ocean between
the Bay of Bengal on the east and the Arabian Sea on the west.

        

India has a unique culture and is one of the oldest and greatest civilizations of the world. It stretches from the snow-capped
Himalayas in the North to the Sun drenched coastal villages of the South. In this section, you will certainly get the best
glimpse of this great country.
